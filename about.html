Hello! My name is Rachel Kim and I'm currently a 4th year at UC Berkeley!

I am majoring in Cognitive Science and with an emphasis in Computational Modeling and minor in Computer Science.
Some of the languages that I am aware of are Python, Java, HTML, CSS, Javascript, and Scratch. Aside from these languages, I have experience working with Django, Dark Sky, Bootstrap, and more. During my free time, some of the things I enjoy to do include playing squash,cooking, watching movies, editing, and taking pictures! To check out some of the pictures that I edited, scroll down below.

Aside from my hobbies, I am currently working two jobs. During the school year, I work as a student-in-charge at the circulation desk for the Bioscience and Natural Resources Library and the Optometry Library. All year around, I work as a coding instructor at ClassADoo, where I teach students from other countries how to code using HTML, CSS, Javascript, and Scratch. After working there for a couple of months, I was promoted from co-instructor to instructor, where I now teach on my own.

The most recent project I worked on was an application called Life as Someone Else (LAS). LAS is a platform that allows for professionals to provide realistic daily details of their work life by writing in diary entries. Students or nonworking individuals will be able to view these entries and gain a more personal perspective of the job.

Check out the home page for more information on the projects that I worked on throughout the years!
