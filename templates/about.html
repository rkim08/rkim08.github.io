Recent Project

The most recent project I worked on was an application called Life as Someone Else (LAS). LAS is a platform that allows for professionals to provide realistic daily details of their work life by writing in diary entries. Students or nonworking individuals will be able to view these entries and gain a more personal perspective of the job. This project was created from scratch and utilized HTML, CSS, Javascript, and Django.
